Biography

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at Victoria University of Wellington, under the supervision of A/Prof. Hui Ma and Aaron Chen. My thesis, recently submitted, focuses on machine learning–driven service optimisation and evolutionary computation for Internet of Things (IoT) applications. I obtained my Master’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from the University of Jinan in 2020, supervised by Prof. Lin Wang, and my Bachelor’s degree in Network Engineering from the same university in 2017.

My research interests center on optimization and its applications, including optimization algorithms, benchmark problem analysis, machine learning for optimization, and optimization in AI security. My current work focuses on applying estimation of distribution algorithms (EDAs) to tackle challenging combinatorial problems, particularly IoT service composition with replication.

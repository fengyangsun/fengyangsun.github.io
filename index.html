Biography

Currently I'm studying a PhD at Victoria University of Wellington since March, 2022. I received my Scientific Engineering Master Degree on Computer Science at University of Jinan in 2020, where I was supervised by Prof. Lin Wang.

My research interests involve everything related to optimization, which include but not limited: optimization algorithm, optimization problem, machine learning, artificial intelligence. I'm working on using neural network to model the distribution of promising solutions to solve a combitorial problem -- the IoT service composition.
